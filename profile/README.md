The hard problems — security, finance, energy, civil resilience — are no longer starved of data; they are **drowning** in it. Advantage now belongs to whoever can integrate open sources, attribute every claim, and put a defensible answer in front of a decision-maker *before the moment passes*. Monarch Castle builds that capability as sovereign, auditable software — owned at home, lawful by design, and accountable to its sources.
